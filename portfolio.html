RQTZ

The purpose of this RQTZ is to provide a space where the user is able to compare two Facebook pages of two different organizations. In order to better online presence, this application seeks to build an understanding about social media best practices and gain lasting impressions in their online presence.

Burger Eater

An interactive menu that allows you to order a new burger, choosing whether or not the burger will be for here or to go. Once you are done eating, you can take your remainder to go! This app remembers your orders and allows you to order time and time again.
